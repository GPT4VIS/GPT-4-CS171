Infographic Critique

The infographic has both positives and negatives. The inforgraphic tries to convey the positions with the top salaries at Google, and what the salaries are for those positions, as shown by the title. Therefore, the intended audience for the infographic seems to be employees/prospective employees for Google, as well as hiring manangers both within and outside of Google. This means that the audience generally has knowledge about what the roles means, and therefore, the design with the job position name and salary works well. The use of quantitative data also helps the user gain specific information from the infographic. Additionally, the addition of the rank next to each position clarifies the infographic and makes it easier to get important information from it quickly. However, the interior design with the overlapping pie chart reflects poor infographic design. The ten different colors overlapping each other with different lines point at them makes it very difficult to keep track of what is being showing in the chart. Overall, this one part of the infographic is too clutter and confusing, affecting an otherwise concise, and easy-to-understand inforgraphic
