Design Critique

Audience: Job seekers and general public interested in salaries at Google.

Questions it answers: What are the top 10 highest-paying positions at Google? What are their respective salary ranges?

Data: Job titles (Ordinal), Salary ranges (Quantitative).

Visual encoding:

  • Job titles: Text labels
  • Salary ranges: Horizontal bars with values shown within bars

Good visualization? Yes, it effectively conveys the information about the top 10 highest-paying positions and their salary ranges at Google. The horizontal bars make it easy to compare the salary ranges across different positions. However, the visualization could be improved by adding more context, such as industry averages or company-wide salary averages for comparison.
